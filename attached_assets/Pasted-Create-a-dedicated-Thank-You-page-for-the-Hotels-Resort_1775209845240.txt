Create a dedicated Thank You page for the “Hotels & Resorts” Recovery Pod landing page.

Goal:
This page appears immediately after a user submits the form on the Hotels & Resorts landing page. The purpose of the Thank You page is to confirm the submission, reinforce the value proposition, and get the lead to book a call through an embedded HubSpot calendar.

Brand:
BH Labs / Recovery Pod

Audience:
Hotel owners, resort operators, hospitality executives, and decision-makers evaluating wellness revenue opportunities.

Tone:
Premium, polished, modern, high-converting, hospitality-focused, clean luxury aesthetic.

Key message from original landing page:
- Turn idle hotel space into $15K+/month wellness revenue
- Zero additional staff, zero marketing required
- Recovery Pod creates a new revenue stream using unused space
- Premium positioning for hotels and resorts

Build the page with these sections:

1. Hero Section
- Large headline: “Thank You — Your Revenue Projection Request Has Been Received”
- Subheadline: “You’re one step closer to turning unused hotel space into a premium wellness revenue stream.”
- Short confirmation text that BH Labs received their information and the next best step is to schedule a strategy call.

2. Booking Section
- Add a strong section headline like: “Book Your Strategy Call”
- Add short copy explaining that on the call, BH Labs will review their property, revenue potential, space requirements, and rollout options.
- Insert a clear placeholder for the HubSpot meeting embed:

3. Value Reinforcement Section
Use 3 to 4 visually clean cards with these benefit themes:
- New revenue from unused space
- Premium guest experience
- Fast payback potential
- Turnkey implementation with training and support

4. What Happens On The Call
Include a short bullet section:
- Review your hotel or resort layout
- Estimate revenue opportunity
- Discuss equipment configuration
- Outline timeline and next steps

5. Trust / Credibility Section
Include a short paragraph referencing BH Labs’ experience:
- 5 successful wellness locations across Miami

  Button text: “Book My Call”

Design and UX requirements:
- Mobile responsive
- Visually consistent with a premium wellness / hotel brand
- Strong spacing, clear hierarchy, modern typography
- Use subtle wellness/luxury styling
- Keep it conversion-focused, not cluttered

- Build as a standalone thank-you page in Replit
- Include smooth scrolling for the “Book My Call” button
- Calendar section should be prominent above the fold or very near it
- Add a fallback text link area under the embed placeholder:
